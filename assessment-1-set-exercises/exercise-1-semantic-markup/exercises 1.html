Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use a substitute like applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup per egg.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C.
  2. Mix the flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Cream together butter, sugars, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs one at a time and mix well.
  5. Gradually mix in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Place spoonfuls of dough onto baking trays.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until golden.
  9. Cool before serving.
